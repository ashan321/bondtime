About Us
At Bond Time, we are a team of Computer Science undergraduates from the
Informatics Institute of Technology, passionate about using technology to support parents
and expecting mothers. Our app offers expert-backed guidance, personalized tips, and
daily prompts to help nurture your child’s growth, from pregnancy through the early years
(0-6). We focus on building strong emotional bonds, fostering brain development, and
encouraging healthy habits, ensuring a positive start to life.
